Lead UX Researcher, University of Michigan · Aug 2023 – Present

User Trust in AI-Driven Virtual Influencers

AI Stimuli Design Causal Inference N = 1,419

The Approach

Executed 6 experiments (N=1,419) testing user responses to realism cues; managed full pipeline from Midjourney image generation to causal modeling.

The Challenge

Mitigating "Uncanny Valley" effect to drive user trust and adoption in AI-generated personas, identifying visual features that move the needle on user trust.

Key Findings

Motion realism increased perceived humanness by 55% for human avatars but had zero impact on AI trust, proving human and AI realism cues are not cross-compatible.

Strategic Outcome

Prevented R&D waste by establishing AI UX Guidelines that prioritize functional authenticity over high-cost, low impact visual realism.

In-Depth Interviews NVivo N = 13
